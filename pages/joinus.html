Join Us!

How to join?

In order to join a meeting, you don't have to be a member. Our meetings are open for everybody, so you're welcome to visit as a guest, no preparations needed! However, you may also want to become an official member - in that case, simply speak to us when you visit, and we'll tell you the details. For the date and time of our next meeting, check the "News" section. We're looking forward to seeing you!

Become an organizer!

The Debate club is made by students, for students. If you want to try being an organizer, this is your chance! We encourage our members to prepare their own presentations, exercises, debate topics, etc., to make our meetings engaging and practical. If you’d like to help us with that, please fill out the following form: *link*

Join our team!

In order to accommodate all the members’ needs, we’re always looking to expand our team. If you believe that your skills can be beneficial to our club, feel free to contact us about it! We’re currently in need of marketers, fact-checkers, and proofreaders, but we’re open for suggestions as well ;)
