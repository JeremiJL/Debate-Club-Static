Why should you join?

    Learn debate skills

    Debate is a crucial skill, which teaches you how to build convincing arguments, stick to your views under stress, withstand scrutiny, and dismantle others' arguments. No matter your vocation, these abilities are essential for success.

    Improve your soft skills

    The Debate club is not only about debating. With us, you'll develop confidence, negotiation, conflict management, critical thinking, active listening, and emotional intelligence. Soft skills such as these are exactly what helps great workers stand out.

    Engaging for everyone

    Teaching debate in a dull, boring way should be considered a crime. Unlike your average Debate club, which consists solely of debate sessions, we offer engaging lectures, diverse exercises, and audience-oriented debates, which will surely keep you interested!

    Educate others

    We believe in the power of knowledge-sharing. Our club provides a supportive environment for students to share ideas, teach each other, and engage in exercises prepared by their fellow members.

    Improve your English

    Proficiency in English is a significant advantage in life, no matter who you are. Joining the Debate club is a good way to practice your English in a work-like setting.

    Connect with others

    Our meetings attract amazing people, making the club a fantastic place to form valuable connections early in life! Who knows, maybe an important acquaintance is just behind the corner?
