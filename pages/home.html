Kaizen Debate Club

”Popularize debate, demonstrate everyone its importance, and teach skills related to it in a fun and engaging way”

What do we do?

During our meetings, we perform a variety of activities: public speaking workshops, negotiation exercises, members-driven lectures, etc. We also conduct debate sessions from time to time, to keep our skills sharp. And to prevent boredom from setting in, we also like to play speaking games together!

Why should you join?

Learn how to debate

Build yours and demolish others’ arguments, all while learning how to withstand stress and scrutiny.

Improve soft skills

Confidence, negotiation, conflict management, critical thinking, emotional intelligence - these skills will surely help you stand out on the job market.

Something for everyone

We conduct public speaking exercises, team games, negotiation workshops, as well as classical debate sessions.

Practice your English

Practice English language in a formal, work-like setting.

Network with others

Meet and connect with various people in our club. Anyone could become your future colleague :)
